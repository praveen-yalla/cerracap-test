Finance/Account/Tax Manager

CerraCap Venture is looking for a finance specialist with minimum of 3 to 5 years of experience with large/medium Private Equity, or Venture Capital in similar capacity. Reporting directly to CEO.

  • Knowledge of ERP systems and related technologies

  • Providing financial models and forecasting
